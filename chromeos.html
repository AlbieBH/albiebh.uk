to unenroll, you need to use quicksilver:

1. go to: Quicksilver and downlaod your board's shim (lenovo 300e chromebooks are 'zork')

2. download chromeos recovery utility on your chrome browser on an unenrolled device

3. open chromeos recovery utility and press the gear icon in the top right - then click 'use local image'. select your downloaded file

4. follow the steps to flash the image to a usb (8GB or larger)

5. on your chromebook, hold down the power button, the refresh key, and the 'esc' key. you should now be in recovery

6. press 'ctrl' and 'd' on your keyboard at the same time, it will prompt you to enter developer mode - confirm and enetr developer mode

7. wait - you should now be on a screen that says 'return to secure mdoe'. press 'esc', refresh and the power keys again.

8. now plug your usb that you prepared earlier into you chromebook, let it do its thing until you see a screen with 'QUICKSILVER' in the top left

9. enter '1' for unenroll, when it says done, unplug your usb and press just the refresh key and the power key

10. when signing in, log in to your non-school account before adding your school account
